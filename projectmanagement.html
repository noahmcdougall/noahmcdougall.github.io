DATA ANALYSIS

(and project management)

Synopsis

Working as an Exploration Geologist at Nyrstar ends up being more project management than 'licking rocks'. During the winter months when it's unfavorable to surface drill, we build the plans and targets for the next year. The rest of the year is spent executing that plan. Since it's a low budget mining operation, I am very hands-on in my projects by necessity. Any given day, I may be preparing a presentation for corporate of our proposed capital budget, only to later dawn my PPE and operate a telehandler (big fork lift) to ensure the logging process doesn't get bottlenecked. It all relies on solid data collection procedures, progress reporting, and data analysis in order to make informed decisions and keep on track. This page highlights some examples of things I've worked on.
